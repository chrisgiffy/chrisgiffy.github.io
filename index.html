Our Story

  • First We Match

    September 2022

    The moment Giffy saw Christy's smile he knew she was special. Christy however matched to see how much of the profile was lies as it matched pefectly all her criteria. When we started talking we felt like teenagers again. We talked for hours and hours and hours.

  • First Date

    Halloween 2022

    After more than a month of talking we finally meet. We decided to meet up in Houston. Giffy went in thinking it was a date, but Christy however, still calls it a casual hangout. We did many different activities that made us comfortable and brought out different sides of us. The date ended at such a high that we decided to meet again the next day. That night we talked over it and decided we should be exclusive.

  • In A Relationship

    November 2nd 2022

    Since then handling a long distance relationship was tough but we made it a point to meet each other atleast once every month. There were many trips to TX and GA

  • Meeting the families

    April 2023

    After 6 months of dating we decided it was time to take our relationship to the next level. We introduce each other to our parent. Our parents welcomed us with open arms, hearts filled with love and awkward conversations!

  • She said yes!

    June 24th 2022

    After months of searching Giffy found the rock that put a glow on Christy's face. With a lot of help from all friends and family we found the perfect venue (The Raibow Lodge, Houston) and were also blessed by the rain Gods to not pour down on us that day. Christy thought she was gatecrashing someones big day but little did she know it was her big day. After a lot of tears and a lot of smiles she said yes! That was surprise #1. We then headed over to Cliff & Libina's house where our family and friends were waiting for the newly engaged couple, which was surprise #2.

  • Church Engagement

    August 19th 2023

    With the blessing of Fr. Binnny Philip at St. Peters Malankara Catholic Church we took the first step towards receiving the sacrement of holy marriage by getting engaged. We were surrounded by our family and friends who showered us with love and blessings.
